Product Description

Inspired by the ancient pottery traditions of the Atlas Mountains, this sculptural vase is hand-thrown by master artisans using locally sourced terra cotta. Each piece undergoes a 48-hour slow-firing process to achieve its unique durability and matte finish.

The geometric motifs are etched by hand before the clay sets, ensuring that no two vases are exactly alike. Its porous nature makes it ideal for dried botanical arrangements or as a standalone statement piece in a modern interior.

  • Dimensions: 30cm H x 18cm W
  • Weight: 1.2kg
  • Material: Moroccan Red Clay
  • Care: Dust with a soft, dry cloth. Do not submerge in water.
